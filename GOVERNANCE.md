# Governance

## Principles

- KISS: Keep it simple. Complexity must justify itself.
- BLUF: Bottom Line Up Front. Lead with the answer.
- Patient safety first: Every technical decision traces to pharmacovigilance outcomes.

## Maintainers

NexVigilant is maintained by Matthew Campion, PharmD — Founder and CEO (matthew@nexvigilant.com).

## Decision Making

- Technical decisions follow the Theory of Vigilance
- Breaking changes require maintainer approval
- Semantic versioning (SemVer) governs all releases

## Code of Conduct

Be professional, be constructive, be respectful. We are building tools that protect patients. Act accordingly.
